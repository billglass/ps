Shyangden Phiroj

Based in Queens, New York, Shyangden Phiroj is a world-renown guitarist. A founding member of one of Nepal’s most famous rock bands, 1974 AD, his playing style implements various genres, including Nepali folk, ragas, rock, funk, blues and jazz.
